Frequently Asked Questions

Go to your Dashboard > Orders. Click on "Track Order" to see the real-time status of your package, from processing to delivery.

When you pay, your money is held safely by NIECOMM. It is only released to the vendor after you have received the item and confirmed it is in good condition (within the 3-day return window).

Register as a vendor and complete your profile. You must provide a valid CAC registration number and a verifiable physical shop address. Our team will verify these details before approving your account.

We offer a 3-day return window for defective or incorrect items. Please review our Return Policy page for full details.
