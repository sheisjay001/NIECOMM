Return Policy

3-Day Return Window

You have 3 days from the date of delivery to inspect your item and request a return if it does not meet your expectations or is defective.

Eligibility for Returns

  • Item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it.
  • Item must be in the original packaging.
  • Receipt or proof of purchase is required.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Perishable goods.
  • Downloadable software products.
  • Personal care items.

Refund Process

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned item. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 7 days.
