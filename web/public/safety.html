Your Safety is Our Priority

How NIECOMM protects you every step of the way.

1. Verified Vendors Only

We don't just let anyone sell on NIECOMM. Every vendor must provide:

  • Valid CAC Registration Number
  • Verifiable Physical Shop Address
  • Government Issued ID

This ensures you are buying from legitimate businesses, not faceless scammers.

2. Escrow Payment Protection

When you pay for an item, the money doesn't go directly to the seller.

Instead, it is held securely by NIECOMM in an escrow account. The vendor is only paid after:

  • You have received the item.
  • You have inspected it and are satisfied.
  • The 3-day return window has passed.

3. Transparent Returns

Not what you expected? No problem.

Since vendors are local (within your state), returning an item is quick and easy. We enforce a strict 3-day return policy for any item that is defective or significantly different from the description.
